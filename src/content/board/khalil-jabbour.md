Dr. Khalil Jabbour is Associate Professor at the Faculty of Medicine of Saint Joseph University, where he serves as Clerkship Director for interns and residents and Director of Third Cycle Studies at the Faculty of Medicine.

He is a specialist in anesthesiology and critical care, with subspecialty expertise in cardiothoracic and vascular anesthesia, cardiac surgical intensive care, and surgical critical care. His clinical interests include cardiac ultrasound, point-of-care ultrasound in critical care, and mechanical ventilation.

Dr. Jabbour is also actively involved in medical simulation and education. He holds a Master's degree in Health Administration.
