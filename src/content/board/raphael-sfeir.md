Dr. Raphael Sfeir is Clinical Instructor of Anesthesiology and Program Director at Saint George University of Beirut and a full-time anesthesiologist at Saint George Hospital Medical Center.

He earned his MD and completed specialty training in Anesthesia and Reanimation at the Lebanese University. He then completed two years of clinical training in France, with a focus on pediatric anesthesia and adult critical care.
