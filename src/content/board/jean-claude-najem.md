Dr. Jean-Claude Najem is an Assistant Professor at the Lebanese University, where he serves as Chair of the Department of Anesthesia and Critical Care. He is also an anesthesiologist at the Lebanese Hospital.

He obtained his Diploma in Anesthesia and Critical Care from the Lebanese University and completed a fellowship at Claude Bernard University in Lyon, France.
