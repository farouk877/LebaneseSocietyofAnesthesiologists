Dr. Carine Zeeni is an Associate Professor of Anesthesia at the American University of Beirut Faculty of Medicine and Medical Center and Director of Neurosurgical Anesthesia at AUBMC. She is a clinician-educator with expertise in neurosurgical anesthesia, perioperative care, patient safety, and simulation-based medical education.

She earned her MD from Saint Joseph University and completed residency training in Anesthesiology and Intensive Care. She then pursued subspecialty fellowship training in Neurosurgical Anesthesiology at Northwestern University, developing expertise in the care of complex, high-risk surgical patients.

At AUB, Dr. Zeeni leads efforts to standardize perioperative care, enhance patient safety, and strengthen collaboration between anesthesia and neurosurgical teams. She has held several institutional leadership roles, including Chair of the Clinical Competency Committee, EPIC Lead, and Chair of the Anesthesia Simulation Committee. A recognized educator and mentor, Dr. Zeeni has been instrumental in shaping residency training and fostering a culture of continuous improvement. Her work extends to research and institutional service, with contributions to peer-reviewed literature and hospital-wide initiatives in quality, informatics, and education.
