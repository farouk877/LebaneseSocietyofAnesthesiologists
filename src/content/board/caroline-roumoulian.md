Dr. Caroline Roumoulian is a full-time anesthesiologist at the Sisters of the Rosary Hospital in Gemmayzeh and Clinical Assistant Professor at the University of Balamand.

She graduated in Anesthesia and Surgical Intensive Care from Saint Joseph University in 1997. She subsequently completed two years of advanced training in obstetric anesthesia at Bichat and Antoine Beclere Hospitals in Paris.

Upon returning to Lebanon in 2000, Dr. Roumoulian began her clinical career at Tannourine Governmental Hospital, where she led the Department of Anesthesia for seven years.
