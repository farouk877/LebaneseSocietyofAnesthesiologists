Dr. Vanda Abi Raad is Clinical Professor and Chair of the Department of Anesthesiology at the Lebanese American University Gilbert and Rose-Marie Chagoury School of Medicine, where she also serves as Associate Dean for Faculty Affairs and Development. She has expertise in anesthesiology, regional anesthesia and pain medicine, simulation-based education, faculty development, continuing professional development, and healthcare leadership.

She received her MD from Saint Joseph University and completed residency training in Anesthesiology at the American University of Beirut Medical Center, followed by fellowship training in cardiovascular anesthesia. She is Arab Board-certified in Anesthesiology and Critical Care and holds a Master of Health Professions Education from the University of Illinois at Chicago and an Executive MBA from LAU.

Dr. Abi Raad serves as a Council Member and member of the Education Committee of the World Federation of Societies of Anaesthesiologists. She is also a Board Member of the Pan Arab Federation of Societies of Anesthesia and a member of its Continuous Professional Development Committee.

In 2025, she was elected an Associate Member of the National Academy of Surgery in France in recognition of her contributions to the field.
